About Me

I am Marwan Mothow, a photographer and videographer with a passion for capturing precious moments and telling stories through the lens of a camera. With over 7 years of experience in photography and videography, I have worked on various projects, from weddings, corporate events, to creative and documentary video productions.

I believe that every image and video has its own unique story. With my expertise in composition, lighting, and editing, I strive to create works that are not only visually beautiful, but also have deep meaning and emotion.
